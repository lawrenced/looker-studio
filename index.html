Executive Summary

This month saw a significant strategic shift in organic performance. While branded search traffic declined, non-branded, high-interest topics drove a solid increase in impressions and conversions. This indicates a growing relevance in more competitive search spaces and less reliance on brand awareness. Our backlink profile continues to grow, and our technical health remains strong. The primary focus for next month will be to address critical technical errors and further optimize content to capture the new non-branded traffic.
